# Restricted Web Content

*DNS settings block whole domains only. Restricted Web Content settings allow you to block subdirectories. However, Restricted Web Content blocks urls from your browser but not your entire system, making it weaker than DNS-level blocks.*

The following are allowed in my **Settings > Screen Time > Content & Privacy > Access to Web Content > Limit Adult Websites > Customize > Restricted** settings. Customize your allowlist to your preferences.

```txt
amazon.com/luna
amazon.com/primevideo
cloud.google.com/dialogflow
```
